Every programmer ought to have a web presence, and this is mine. Welcome.

Currently, I'm a senior security engineer doing research at Trail of Bits. I work on SIEVE, a project aimed at reinventing the process of vulnerability disclosure using zero-knowledge proofs. Quite recently, I worked on cROPcircle, a Binary Ninja plugin that helps users build and visualize ROP chains. Hopefully it will be open-sourced very soon. In the past, I studied at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I did research in the Network Security Research Group (NSRG). In my free time, I enjoy Capture The Flag, Blacksmithing, Graphics/VFX, and going to information security conferences.
